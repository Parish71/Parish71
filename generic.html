Structured and Data-Driven Thinking

In today's world, where information is abundant and decisions are often data-driven, adopting a structured thinking approach is essential. Just as Google teaches through Google Analytics, I recognize the importance of systematically and objectively analyzing information to make informed and effective decisions. Structured thinking enables me to assess challenges with clarity, identify patterns and trends, and implement solutions based on solid evidence. By incorporating this mindset into my work, I aim not only to achieve results but also to optimize processes and maximize the impact of my actions. With a commitment to ongoing data analysis and a structured approach, I am empowered to tackle the challenges of the digital world intelligently and innovatively.

Eudaemonia and the Essence of Human Being

The philosophy of eudaemonia, based on Sartre's existentialism and the premise of NLP that "if one human being was capable of doing it, everyone is," guides my personal and professional journey. For me, eudaemonia goes beyond the pursuit of fleeting pleasures or material success; it is about realizing the deepest and most authentic human potential. Inspired by the belief that each individual possesses a unique and invaluable essence, I strive to cultivate a sense of purpose and meaning in all my endeavors. I recognize the radical freedom of choice that each of us possesses and embrace the responsibility to create my own existence with integrity and authenticity. By embracing the philosophy of eudaemonia, I commit to seeking not only superficial happiness but rather a fulfilling life connected to my deepest values and positively contributing to the world around me.
